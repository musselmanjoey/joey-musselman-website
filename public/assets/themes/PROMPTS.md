# Tips for Best Results

1. **Generate at 2x size** then scale down for crisp edges (e.g., 1600x800 for sky)
2. **Consistent lighting** - keep light source direction the same (upper right)
3. **Remove backgrounds** - use remove.bg or Photoshop for transparent PNGs
4. **Regenerate freely** - AI results vary, keep trying until you get the vibe
5. **Color match** - if colors clash, note hex codes and ask AI to match
