# Starfall over Velaris - Scene Prompt v1

## Book Reference
**A Court of Mist and Fury** by Sarah J. Maas - Starfall event, Night Court

## Book Descriptions (for accuracy)
- Starfall: Annual event where "spirits" migrate across the sky (not actual stars)
- Viewed from a balcony of the House of Wind
- Stars wheel by "close and swift" - thousands until dawn
- Velaris: "City of Starlight" with white marble buildings
- The Sidra River runs through the city
- Rolling hills, music and dancing on patios below
- Stars leave "glowing dust" when they touch things
- Deep night sky with purple/indigo tones (Night Court aesthetic)

## Image Generation Prompt (Gemini)

```
(wide shot:1.3), nighttime balcony view overlooking magical fantasy city, white marble buildings glowing softly below, winding river reflecting starlight, deep indigo and purple night sky, countless stars visible, a few shooting stars with glowing trails streaking across sky, ornate stone balcony railing in foreground, distant rolling hills, warm lights from windows and patios below, magical ethereal atmosphere, romantic fantasy scene, city of starlight aesthetic, dreamy soft lighting, photorealistic, 8k, fantasy book cover quality
```

## Requirements
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (1920x1080 or higher)
- Key elements:
  - Dark night sky (deep indigo/purple)
  - White/pale marble city below
  - River visible (the Sidra)
  - Balcony perspective (viewing from above)
  - Some static stars in sky
  - Warm glowing lights from the city
  - Magical, romantic atmosphere

## Effect Pairing
- **Primary effect**: Shooting stars with glowing trails
- **Config**: white stars, lavender trails, purple glow, frequent spawning

## Scene Context
This is Starfall - the annual event in Velaris where spirits migrate across the sky. One of the most romantic scenes in ACOMAF, where Feyre and Rhysand watch from a balcony of the House of Wind.

## Notes
- The shooting star effect will add the dynamic movement
- Base image should have a relatively clear sky area for stars to streak across
- City lights below create contrast with dark sky
