- **Primary effect**: Water drips - droplets falling from stalactites

- **Config**: Cyan/blue colored drips, very subtle green-tinted mist

## Scene Context
This is a hidden temple to Umberlee (D&D sea goddess) built into a coastal cave. Followers come here to make offerings and pray for safe passage at sea. Water drips constantly from stalactites overhead, creating ripples in sacred tide pools. The cave glows with bioluminescent algae, giving everything an otherworldly blue-green hue.
