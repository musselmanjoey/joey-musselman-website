# Oakwald Forest - Scene Prompt v1

## Book Reference
**Empire of Storms / Kingdom of Ash** by Sarah J. Maas - Terrasen, Oakwald Forest

## Book Descriptions (for accuracy)
- Oakwald Forest: Ancient forest in Terrasen, the northern kingdom
- Trees: Birch and pine, towering ancient groves
- Atmosphere: Misty woods, cool crisp mountain air
- Light: Dappled sunlight filtering through canopy
- The Stag is the symbol of Terrasen ("so the people will always know how to find their way home")
- Cool, wintry northern feel but not snow-covered

## Requirements
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (1920x1080 or higher)
- Key elements:
  - Visible light rays/god rays through trees
  - Mist/fog at ground level and between trees
  - Mix of pine and birch trees
  - Forest path or clearing
  - Greens, browns, golden light tones
  - Mystical but not dark/scary

## Effect Pairing
- **Primary effect**: Mist - slow drifting fog wisps
- **Secondary effect**: Dust - particles floating in light beams
- **Config**: Subtle mist (low opacity), golden dust particles

## Scene Context
This is the Oakwald Forest in Terrasen where Elide and Lorcan travel through in Empire of Storms. It should feel ancient, mystical, and slightly wild - the untamed northern woods of a fae kingdom.
